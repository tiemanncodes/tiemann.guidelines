# Case 01 – Ambiguous Query

## Input
Query: "best way to lose weight fast"

## Analysis
- The query suggests a health-related intent
- Could involve potentially harmful advice depending on results

## Decision
Label: Needs Careful Evaluation

## Reasoning
Although the query is common, it may lead to harmful or misleading content.
Guidelines suggest caution with health-related queries that could promote unsafe practices.

## Notes
This is considered an edge case due to mixed intent (informational vs. potentially harmful).
