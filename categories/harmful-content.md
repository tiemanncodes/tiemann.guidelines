# Harmful Content Classification

---

## Definition

Content that may cause physical, psychological, or societal harm.

---

## High-Risk Domains

- Health
- Safety
- Legal / financial systems

---

## Types of Harm

### 1. Direct Harm
- Instructions for unsafe actions

### 2. Indirect Harm
- Misinformation
- Misleading advice

### 3. Circumvention
- Bypassing safeguards (medical, legal)

---

## Evaluation Criteria

- Intent (explicit vs. implicit)
- Severity of potential harm
- Likelihood of misuse

---

## Edge Cases

- Educational vs. actionable harmful content
- Awareness vs. instruction

---

## Decision Principle

When risk is non-negligible, apply conservative classification.
