# Annotation Methodology

My annotation process follows three main steps:

1. Understand the guideline
- Identify key rules and constraints
- Define what is considered acceptable vs. not

2. Analyze the content
- Determine intent
- Identify potential ambiguity

3. Make a decision
- Apply the most relevant rule
- Ensure consistency with similar cases

4. Justify the decision
- Provide clear reasoning
- Reference guideline principles
